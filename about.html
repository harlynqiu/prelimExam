Harlyn Nichole T. Qiu

Most often, when asking who “Harlyn Nichole Qiu” is, not much can say. The reason for this is because she likes to keep things to herself and only allows a certain number of people to get to know her. Born and raised in Davao City, Philippines, Harlyn is the eldest among her siblings. She is raised in a Chinese household so her views and practices follow the Chinese standards. As the eldest among the siblings, she bears the responsibilities of an “atsi” (Chinese term for sister).
